About

I have recently completed my B.Tech in Computer Science from IIIT Bhubaneswar where I worked with Prof. Swati Vipsita on solving problems in Computational Biology using Machine Learning.
There I mainly worked on improvising the learnability of different neural network architecture for micro-array cancer classification. During the course of my B.Tech, I worked at Quantiphi Analytics Inc. as a winter intern. There I worked mainly on the usecase of textless elements where the task was to identify frames with overlaid text(credits, dialogues) present in a video clip and identify the corresponding textless frame present at the end of the video clip.

Research Work

Cancer Classification Using Extreme Learning Machine

In this paper, a novel hybrid technique is proposed where Jaya optimization along with Adaptive simulated annealing is used for parameter optimization of Extreme Learning Machine(ELM) model. The efficiency of the model is verified against other existing techniques.
(Speaker Presentation)

Revisiting Radial Basis Function Networks(RBFN) for Cancer Classification

Efficiently designed the model for improving the learning of RBFN model parameters using Jaya optimization technique. This analysis brings other ways through which efficiency of this model can be increased on the task of cancer classification over the existing learning techniques.

Analysing the RBFN model and its learning techniques

Implemented and analyzed the traditional RBFN model and it's existing learning paradigms which include Three-step learning, Gradient Learning, and Genetic Algorithm. This gave an insight into the working of the model and further areas of improvement.

Hybrid of PCA-GA for dimensionality reduction and feature selection of Probabilistic Neural Networks(PNN)

Implemented and analyzed a hybrid approach of PCA-GA, PCA(principal component Analysis) and GA(Genetic Algorithm) are used for extracting relevant features and PNN(Probabilistic Neural Networks) is used as a classifier and further GA is implemented to get the optimized PNN architecture.

Other Work Experience
